Refund Policy

Eligibility for Refund

To be eligible for a refund, the following conditions must be met:
1. Return Window: The product must be returned within 48 hours of the original shipment date from our shop
2. Product Condition: The product must be returned in the same condition it was sent in, with all original packaging, tags, and accessories included.

Refund Details

If the above conditions are met, we will process a full refund of the product's purchase price. However, please note that.
- Refunds do not include waybill or dispatch rider fees.
- You will only receive a refund for the product's purchase price, excluding any shipping or delivery charges.
Refund Process
We will process the refund to the original payment method once we receive and verify the returned product.
Non-Refundable Situations
If the product is returned after 48 hours or in a condition other than as sent, we reserve the right to refuse the return and refund.

Note: At Yarima's Collections, we strive to ensure our customers are satisfied with their purchases. However, we understand that sometimes returns may be necessary.
